Game Instructions

Objective: The game is an endless runner where players are tasked with collecting "Our Way" behaviors.
Points System: There are 12 correct behaviors that award +10 points each when collected. Conversely, there are 12 incorrect behaviors that deduct -5 points each.
Gameplay: Each player can participate once from a laptop.
Game Control: Move Left: Left-Arrow
Move Right: Right-Arrow
Jump: Up-Arrow
Goal: Aim to collect all 12 correct behaviors to fill the collectible bar located at the top right corner of the screen, thereby maximizing your score.
Score Calculation: The total score is based on the sum of all collected items, both correct and incorrect.
Final Score: Your final score will be displayed at the end of your journey.
Technical Support: If you encounter any issues, please reach out to our support team.
