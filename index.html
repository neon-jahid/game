Game Instructions

Objective: The game is an endless runner where players collect "Our Way" behaviors.
Points System: 12 correct behaviors = +10 points each, 12 incorrect behaviors = -5 points each.
Gameplay: Each player can participate once from a laptop.
Game Controls: Move Left: Left-Arrow
Move Right: Right-Arrow
Jump: Up-Arrow
Goal: Collect all 12 correct behaviors to fill the collectible bar at the top right and maximize your score.
Final Score: Displayed at the end of the game.
